# Image generation prompts

Mode: built-in Imagegen with magenta chroma-key background and local alpha extraction.

## Shared prompt

Use case: stylized-concept. Asset type: one standalone production-ready game-piece sprite for a browser-based Battleship game. Create exactly one modern military naval vessel, fully visible and isolated, in mathematically strict orthographic 90-degree top-down view. Horizontal orientation, stern on the left, bow pointing right. Center precisely with generous padding. Uniform neutral overhead lighting. Razor-sharp cutout contour. Serious realistic cyber-military marine command-center aesthetic; dark gray steel, anthracite and black armor, cold turquoise reflections, restrained neon-green technical lights, subtle wear; plausible naval engineering. Perfectly flat solid #ff00ff chroma-key background, with no gradient, texture, reflection, floor, lighting variation, glow or shadow. Never use magenta on the vessel. No text, numbers, labels, logo, watermark, UI, grid lines, ocean, water, wake, external shadow, perspective, angled camera, side view, people, aircraft, helicopters, smoke, explosion, cropping, blur, cartoon or fantasy style.

## Length proportion revision

The aircraft carrier and floating fortress were edited using their earlier chroma-source images as edit targets. Only the proportions and the hull/deck sections affected by the extension were changed.

- Aircraft carrier: extend the flight deck and hull longitudinally by roughly 40 percent, slightly reduce the beam, retain strict top-down view, empty deck, offset island, materials and lighting; produce an unmistakably long silhouette that clearly exceeds every 4-grid ship.
- Floating fortress: extend the armored hull and deck corridors longitudinally by roughly 35 percent, slightly reduce the visual beam, retain the central citadel, turrets, VLS banks, strict top-down view, materials and lighting; make it clearly and dramatically longer than the 5-grid carrier while retaining the 7x2 gameplay footprint.
